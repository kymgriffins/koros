01 — Manifesto

Why we exist

Kenya's national budget exceeds KSh 4 trillion annually. County governments control another KSh 500 billion. Yet for most young citizens, these numbers remain abstract—trapped in PDFs, buried in jargon, disconnected from daily life.

The Problem

Budget documents are designed for accountants, not citizens. Critical spending decisions happen in opaque corridors, far from public scrutiny.

Our Response

We decode complexity. We map the money trail from Treasury to township. We equip you with evidence to demand answers.

02 — Method
